Therefore, it is mandatory to get your air conditioner serviced at least 2 times in a year with periodic inspection. Moreover, AC Overhauling also known as ac master service is essential every year for peak performance.

This is a very common problem in Pakistan where consumers do not believe in ac maintenance rather than ac repair. Consumers will only get their ac serviced when there is a problem with their air conditioner which is why most air conditioners fail and have to be replaced. Therefore, it is better to perform ac service on regular basis rather than get your ac repaired.

Some of the most common problems even with a brand new air conditioner is that its not cooling properly or there is a water leakage issue. This is a very common ac installation issue which suggests that the ac is not properly installed which causes water leakage from ac indoor. Moreover, if the ac is not cooling that means that the gas is leaking from the flare and it needs to be properly tightened.
Therefore, it is essential to get your ac installed from a proper AC Servicing Company that can provide you with a warranty in case if there is any issue with the ac installation job.

AC cooling is directly linked with the refrigerant. If there is low refrigerant the cooling will be affected. Low Refrigerant suggests that the ac has got a leakage which then needs to be inspected thoroughly. Most common problems of ac not cooling is a leaked condenser or evaporator coil, dirty air filter or wrong settings on your remote.

Adding ac refrigerant after every 6 or 12 months suggests that your air conditioner is leaking and it needs repair. Dont add refrigerant just like that. Get your evaporator, condenser coils, u-band and piping thoroughly checked. Leaking refrigerant will badly affect the cooling performance of the ac and it will consume more electricity. Therefore, ac gas charging should not be refilled more often as it suggests that your ac needs to be diagnosed first to identify the cause of gas leakage.

One of the most common causes of a brand new ac not cooling is a bad installation job. AC Installers usually dont tighten the flare nut properly and the gas starts leaking slowly which results in low cooling or no cooling at all.

Water Leakage from the ac indoor unit is one of the most common problems that you will find in Pakistan. Water leakage from ac indoor unit suggests that either the drain pipe is choked, there is a refrigerant leak or an installation fault which can be rectified.

One of the most common issues of ac gas leakage is leakage from an indoor coil also known as an evaporator coil. 80% of leakage is from the evaporator coil and there can be a lot of reasons for refrigerant leakage. It can be corrosion, lack of ac servicing, or VOC also known as volatile organic compounds which are found in the room where the indoor coil is installed and that can be the paint, carpet, or heavy degreasers which have very high fumes that can cause pitting in the copper piping ultimately leading to leakage in the evaporator coil. Another common reason for refrigerant leakage especially in Karachi is moisture, humidity which has a huge impact on the copper evaporator and condenser coil. The only solution to this problem is frequent ac service which can improve the life cycle of the air conditioner
